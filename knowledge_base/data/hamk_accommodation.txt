SECTION 1: GENERAL INFORMATION
----------------------------------------------------------

HAMK University of Applied Sciences operates across seven campuses in Finland.
Accommodation options vary by campus — some campuses offer on-campus student
housing managed directly by HAMK, while others rely on external housing partners
and private rental markets.

IMPORTANT RULE: You can only apply for student accommodation at the campus where
you are currently studying. Do not apply for accommodation at a different campus.

The seven campus cities are:
- Hämeenlinna
- Riihimäki
- Forssa
- Valkeakoski
- Evo
- Lepaa
- Mustiala

Campuses with on-campus HAMK-managed housing: Evo, Lepaa, Mustiala
Campuses relying on external/city housing partners: Hämeenlinna, Riihimäki,
Forssa, Valkeakoski

----------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: CAMPUS OVERVIEWS
----------------------------------------------------------

--- Hämeenlinna Campus ---
Hämeenlinna is the main HAMK campus. Students can apply for housing through
the Hämeenlinna Region Student Housing Foundation (HOPS). HOPS offers
apartments in the Visakylä, Kampuskaarre, and Hattelmala areas. Utilities are
included in the rent.

Family housing: Hämeenlinnan Asunnot provides family-suitable rental apartments.

Private rental options: Vuokraovi, Oikotie, NAL, Lumo, Joo-kodit.

Note: Applications to HOPS are valid for only 3 months. Do not apply too early.

--- Riihimäki Campus ---
Students can apply for housing through HOPS, which offers apartments in the
Peltosaari area with utilities included. Riihimäen Kotikulma provides options
for both individual students and families.

Private rental options are also available through Vuokraovi and Oikotie.

Note: The same 3-month application validity rule applies as in Hämeenlinna.

--- Forssa Campus ---
Forssa does not have dedicated student dormitories. Degree students are expected
to find housing in the private or public rental market.

City-owned option: Forssa Asunnot Oy — available for both individuals and families.
Private options: Vuokraovi, Oikotie.

--- Valkeakoski Campus ---
City-owned option: Valkeakoski Asunnot Oy — available for individuals and families.
Private options: Vuokraovi, Oikotie.

--- Evo Campus ---
Evo is located in a beautiful lakeside setting surrounded by the forests of the
Rautjärvi lakes. It is a remote campus with no public transportation available.
Students living here typically need their own transportation.

HAMK manages on-campus housing at Evo. Only individual students can apply
(no family accommodation available).

--- Lepaa Campus ---
Lepaa is a historic campus set in a park-like environment. HAMK manages
on-campus housing for individual students. Family accommodation is not available.

--- Mustiala Campus ---
Mustiala is another historic park-campus with a relaxed rural atmosphere.
Amenities include a gym, a leisure room, and a lakeside sauna. HAMK manages
on-campus housing for individual students only. Family accommodation is not available.

SECTION 3: ROOM TYPES AND PRICES
----------------------------------------------------------

All prices listed are per person per month (long-term) or per person per night
(short-term), unless otherwise noted.

--- EVO CAMPUS ---

Long-term shared apartments (furnished):
  - Rent: €180 – €275 / month / person
  - Furnishings included: bed, desk, chair, wardrobe
  - Shared kitchen and living area with other residents

Long-term studio or one-bedroom apartments:
  - Rent: €300 – €385 / month / apartment
  - Fewer furnishings included
  - More privacy than shared apartments

Short-term accommodation:
  - Rate: €25 – €35 / night / person
  - Bedding and kitchen utensils provided

--- LEPAA CAMPUS ---

Long-term shared apartments (furnished):
  - Rent: €199 – €295 / month / person

Long-term studio or one-bedroom apartments (unfurnished):
  - Rent: €295 – €560 / month / apartment

Short-term accommodation:
  - Rate: €30 – €40 / night / person
  - Note: Guests must bring their own bed linens

--- MUSTIALA CAMPUS ---

Long-term shared apartments (furnished):
  - Rent: €180 – €340 / month / person

Long-term studio or one-bedroom apartments (unfurnished):
  - Rent: €337 – €495 / month / apartment

Short-term accommodation:
  - Rate: €25 – €35 / night / person
  - Special package: "Cow Shed Week" — 4 nights for €85 (unique Mustiala experience)

--- HÄMEENLINNA / RIIHIMÄKI (via HOPS) ---

Exact prices are set by HOPS and may vary. Utilities are included in rent.
Contact HOPS directly or visit their website for current pricing.

--- FORSSA / VALKEAKOSKI (via city housing companies) ---

Prices are set by local housing providers (Forssa Asunnot Oy, Valkeakoski
Asunnot Oy). Contact them directly for current availability and pricing.

SECTION 4: APPLICATION PROCESS
----------------------------------------------------------

ON-CAMPUS HOUSING (Evo, Lepaa, Mustiala):

1. A single application form covers both short-term and long-term accommodation
   at each campus.
2. Submit the application form available on the HAMK accommodation webpage.
3. Apply only for the campus where you are enrolled as a student.
4. Wait for confirmation from the campus accommodation coordinator.
5. Sign the rental agreement and pay any required deposit before arrival.

To terminate or pause your rental agreement:
  - Use the official form to terminate your agreement.
  - You can also apply to change your agreement status to "half-rent" temporarily
    (e.g., during a long internship period or exchange semester).

OFF-CAMPUS HOUSING (Hämeenlinna, Riihimäki, Forssa, Valkeakoski):

1. Contact the relevant housing provider directly (HOPS, city housing company,
   or private landlord).
2. Use their website or contact form to apply.
3. For HOPS: Note that applications are valid for only 3 months — do not apply
   too early before your intended move-in date.
4. For private rentals: Search on Vuokraovi.com or Oikotie.fi.

GENERAL TIPS:
- Start your housing search as early as possible, especially for popular campuses.
- International students arriving for the first time should contact the
  accommodation coordinator well in advance of arrival.
- If you have special needs or accessibility requirements, mention them in your
  application.

SECTION 6: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)
----------------------------------------------------------

Q: Can I apply for accommodation at a campus that is not my study campus?
A: No. HAMK policy requires that you apply for accommodation only at the campus
   where you are currently enrolled as a student.

Q: Can families with children get accommodation at HAMK campuses?
A: On-campus housing at Evo, Lepaa, and Mustiala is available for individual
   students only — families are not accommodated there. For family housing in
   Hämeenlinna and Riihimäki, contact Hämeenlinnan Asunnot or Riihimäen
   Kotikulma respectively. Forssa and Valkeakoski also have family-suitable
   options through their city housing companies.

Q: Are utilities included in the rent?
A: For HOPS-managed apartments (Hämeenlinna, Riihimäki), utilities are included.
   For on-campus housing (Evo, Lepaa, Mustiala), check your rental agreement —
   shared apartments typically include utilities. Studio/one-bedroom units may
   not include all utilities.

Q: How far in advance should I apply for accommodation?
A: For HOPS housing, note that applications are only valid for 3 months, so
   applying too early is not helpful. For on-campus housing at Evo, Lepaa, and
   Mustiala, it is advisable to apply as soon as you have confirmed your study
   place, especially for the autumn semester (August start).

Q: Is short-term accommodation available if I arrive before my long-term place
   is ready?
A: Yes. Evo, Lepaa, and Mustiala offer short-term (nightly) accommodation.
   Rates range from €25–€40/night depending on the campus. This is suitable
   for orientation weeks or while waiting for a long-term contract to begin.

Q: Are the on-campus rooms furnished?
A: Shared apartments at Evo and Mustiala include basic furniture (bed, desk,
   chair, wardrobe). Studio and one-bedroom apartments at Lepaa, Mustiala, and
   Evo are typically unfurnished or have fewer furnishings. Always confirm with
   the campus coordinator before signing.

Q: What is the "Cow Shed Week" at Mustiala?
A: Mustiala Campus offers a unique short-term accommodation package called
   "Cow Shed Week" — 4 nights for €85. It is a distinctive rural experience
   reflecting Mustiala's agricultural heritage.

Q: How do I cancel or pause my rental agreement?
A: Use the official termination or half-rent change form available on the HAMK
   accommodation webpage. A "half-rent" status is possible when you are away
   for an extended period, such as during an internship or exchange semester.

Q: Is there public transportation near the on-campus housing?
A: Availability varies significantly. Evo Campus is in a remote forest location
   with no public transportation — a car is strongly recommended. Lepaa and
   Mustiala are also in rural settings with limited transport links. Hämeenlinna
   and Riihimäki have better public transport connections.

Q: Can exchange or visiting students apply for accommodation?
A: Yes, both degree and exchange students can apply for accommodation. Contact
   arrival@hamk.fi for guidance on the process as an international or exchange
   student.

Q: What should I do if I have accessibility or special accommodation needs?
A: Mention your requirements clearly in your application form or contact the
   accommodation coordinator at your campus directly via email before applying.

Q: I'm studying in Hämeenlinna. Where exactly are the HOPS apartments located?
A: HOPS offers apartments in three areas near Hämeenlinna: Visakylä,
   Kampuskaarre, and Hattelmala. All are close to the main HAMK campus.

Q: Can I find a private rental apartment instead of using HOPS or HAMK housing?
A: Yes. Private rentals are available through platforms such as Vuokraovi.com,
   Oikotie.fi, NAL, Lumo, and Joo-kodit. These are especially useful in cities
   like Forssa and Valkeakoski where dedicated student housing is limited.
